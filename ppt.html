Our Solution: The Flight Difficulty Score

We developed a weighted scoring model to quantify operational complexity. The score is calculated daily, allowing for dynamic resource allocation.

Actionable Recommendations

1. Deploy a 'Turnaround Taskforce' for STL

**Action:** For the top 5 daily 'Difficult' flights to STL, pre-assign an expanded ground crew to guarantee the aircraft is ready for boarding ahead of schedule, directly countering the **-51% ground time pressure**.

2. Implement a 'Priority Transfer' Protocol

**Action:** For STL-bound flights, baggage handlers should prioritize sorting and loading transfer bags first. Since these flights handle **50% more transfers**, this targeted approach will reduce mishandled bags and departure delays.

Data-Driven Decisions
